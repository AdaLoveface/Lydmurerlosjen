P1 Project Requirements September 18, 2016

Halvor Schjelderup, Magnus Einan Mikalsen, Mats Nordaas Olsen and Ada Mathea Hoel

The client: Ada Mathea Hoel

Ada studies music technology at NTNU, and is interested in making a new website for her student union, Lydmurerlosjen.

Purpose and goals of the website

The goals are to promote the activities of Lydmurerlosjen and also have a private part of the website for the existing members to distribute info and events.

The website's audience

The audience for this website will be both people looking for basic information about the union with an overview of what the union is up to with activities people can join, and a private part of the website which is intended for the members of Lydmurerlosjen with inside information where they also can discuss activities and future plans for the union.

Content of the website

Events

This section describes upcoming events that Lydmurerlosjen will be hosting and how you can participate in these events.

About

This section describes what Lydmurerlosjen's purpose is, and who is part of the board, possibly with pictures of them and a description of what part of the union they are responsible for, and all of their email addresses.

Contact

This section will an interactive Google map, a telephone number, email address, links to our social media presence, and any other useful info to get in touch with the union.

Lydmurer

This is the insider section where members of the union can log in to find insider information only intended for the members, including member-only events, a feedback section where they can discuss different aspects of how the union is ruled and suggestions for future changes and information related to music technology.
