Halvor Schjelderup, Magnus Einan Mikalsen, Mats Nordaas Olsen og Ada Mathea Hoel
September 14, 2016

The client: Ada Mathea Hoel

Ada studies music technology at NTNU, and is interested in making a new website for her student union, Lydmurerlosjen.

Purpose and goals of the website

The goals are to promote the activities of Lydmurerlosjen and also have a private part of the website for our existing members to distribute info and events.

The website's audience

The audience for this website will be divided in two: people looking for basic information about the union and an overview of what the union is up to with activities people can join. The other, private part of the website is intended for the members of Lydmurerlosjen, where they can discuss activities and future plans for the union.

Content of the Website

  • Events
    This section describes upcoming events that Lydmurerlosjen will be hosting and how you can participate in these events.
  • About
    This section describes what Lydmurerlosjen's purpose is, and who is part of the board, possibly with pictures of them and a description of what part of the union they are responsible for, and all of their email addresses.
  • Contact
    This section will contain an interactive Google map, a telephone number, email address, links to our social media presence, and any other useful info to get in touch with the union.
